The Problem

Why engineers need faster access to case context

⏱️ Time Wasted

Engineers spend 15–30 minutes reading through long ServiceNow case timelines before understanding the current status of a ticket.

🔀 Context Switching

During Webex calls and escalations, engineers must leave the conversation, open ServiceNow, find the case, and manually read through comments, work notes, and emails.

🔁 Repeated Work

Without a quick summary, engineers repeat diagnostic steps that were already tried, wasting time and frustrating customers.

📊 Scale of the Problem

  • Average case has 10–30+ journal entries
  • Customer emails add another 5–15 messages
  • Engineers handle 5–10 cases/day
  • ~2 hours/day spent just reading case history

The Solution

AI-powered case summaries delivered where engineers work — Webex

💬 How It Works

  • Engineer types a case number in Webex (e.g., CS0001026)
  • Bot instantly shows a "Generating summary…" card
  • Fetches case data, journal entries, and emails from ServiceNow
  • Sends the full timeline to CIRCUIT LLM for summarization
  • Delivers a clean, structured summary card in 5–8 seconds

📋 Summary Includes

  • Problem — What is broken and who is affected
  • Root Cause — If identified in work notes
  • What Was Done — Actions taken (deduplicated)
  • Current Status — Where the ticket stands now
  • Next Steps — If explicitly mentioned

Differentiation

How this compares to ServiceNow's built-in AI Assist
🏢 ServiceNow AI Assist (Built-in)
  • Lives inside ServiceNow UI only
  • Requires ServiceNow login & license
  • Works on Incidents (INC)
  • Read-only widget on the ticket page
  • Must already be on the ticket to see it
  • Not accessible during Webex calls
🤖 Our Webex Summarization Bot
  • Works inside Webex — where engineers collaborate
  • No ServiceNow login needed
  • Works on CSM Cases (CS/TASK) — extensible to INC
  • Interactive Adaptive Cards with actions
  • Quick lookup during meetings & escalations
  • Shareable — whole team sees the summary

🤝 Complementary, Not Competing

SN AI Assist = deep dive on the ticket page  |  Our Bot = quick context during collaboration

Integration into Existing Flow

How this fits into current CX operations

🔗 Webex Connect Integration

Embed the bot as a node in Webex Connect flows. When a case is created or escalated, auto-post a summary to the team space.

📢 Escalation Alerts

When a case is escalated to a higher tier, automatically generate and post a summary to the receiving team's Webex space — no manual lookup needed.

🔄 Shift Handoff

At shift change, engineers can quickly summarize all their active cases and share in a Webex space for the incoming shift.

📊 Manager Dashboard

Managers can request summaries of any case during stand-ups without opening ServiceNow — saves meeting time.

🎫 Incident Support (INC)

Extend to Incidents (INC) table in addition to CSM cases — same architecture, just query a different ServiceNow table.

📱 Webex Spaces

Add the bot to team spaces. Any team member can type a case number and everyone sees the summary — great for war rooms.
